3. How The Extension Works

The extension operates entirely within your browser:

  1. You visit the Amazon Associates dashboard and log in with your own credentials
  2. The extension captures authentication headers from your existing session
  3. It uses these headers to make requests directly to Amazon's API on your behalf
  4. Sales data is displayed in the extension popup and cached locally
  5. Notifications are generated locally when new sales are detected

All communication happens directly between your browser and Amazon. We are not in the middle.

4. Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions and here's exactly why:

alarms

Schedules automatic checks for new sales data every 15 minutes (configurable). No data is transmitted.

notifications

Displays browser notifications when new sales are detected. Notifications are generated locally.

cookies

Reads your existing Amazon Associates session cookies to authenticate API requests. We don't create, modify, or transmit cookies.

storage

Stores cached sales data, license key, and preferences in your browser's local storage. Data never leaves your device.

scripting

Injects scripts into Amazon Associates pages to intercept API requests and capture authentication headers. This is necessary to make authenticated requests to Amazon's API.

tabs

Manages browser tabs to open/refresh the Amazon Associates page when needed for authentication. Required for background data fetching.

host_permissions (https://associados.amazon.com.br/*)

Allows the extension to access only the Amazon Associates Brazil domain. This is the ONLY external domain the extension communicates with, and it's Amazon's official domain.

5. Third-Party Services

Amazon Associates

The extension communicates directly with Amazon's official API at associados.amazon.com.br. All requests are made using your existing authentication. We comply with Amazon Associates Terms of Service.

Stripe (Payment Processing)

For PRO license purchases, we use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe may collect payment information according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to your full credit card details.

No Other Third Parties

We don't use analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.), tracking pixels, or any other third-party services.

6. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's encrypted storage API
  • Communication with Amazon uses HTTPS encryption
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • License keys are stored locally and only validated client-side (in V1 MVP)

7. Data Retention

Data is retained locally in your browser until:

  • You uninstall the extension
  • You clear your browser data
  • Cache expires (2 minutes for today's data, 30 minutes for historical data)

We have no control over or access to this data.

Summary in Plain English

Your data is YOUR data.

This extension works like a personal assistant that lives in your browser. It helps you check your Amazon Associates dashboard automatically, but it never reports back to us. We literally cannot see your sales data, earnings, or any other information because it never leaves your browser.

Think of it like a calculator app on your phone - it does calculations for you, but the calculator company doesn't know what numbers you're crunching. Same principle here.

Privacy-first isn't a feature for us - it's the architecture.
